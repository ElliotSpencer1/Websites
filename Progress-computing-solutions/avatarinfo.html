Avatar information

The Avatar page in which you have come from allows you to get items for your character, this includes hats, clothing and glasses.


How do i get items?


All of the items have their own price so called in order to gain these items you can hover over them and see the amount of score you would need in a certain section to get the item.


How do i equip them?


You must be careful when scrolling through the items as they equip with one click and also unequip with one click.
